Funding options for your small-medium size business

You need funding to grow your business to the next level, or to meet critical obligations. But you may not want to wait for a bank business loan to be approved, or – like many small or mid-sized businesses, you may not easily qualify for traditional business loan offerings. Don’t worry, we’re here to help.

One Park Financial believes that your business should have ready access to working capital, just like big corporations do. So, we pair you with funders that focus on working with small and mid-sized businesses, and are happy to work with people who may not have a perfect credit score.

Funding options that we can help you access include:

  • FAST REVENUE
    BASED BUSINESS FUNDING

    This working capital product type is referred to as a Merchant Cash Advance or MCA for short. This is a factor-based type of transaction where you will receive the working capital you need FAST, and your repayment will be calculated based upon the factor value of the terms of your transaction.
