Earlier this month, One Park Financial partnered with The United Way to spend an afternoon giving back to their local community. Team Grovies visited Golden Glades Elementary in Opa Locka, FL to help spruce up the school. Grovies broke off into groups to help sand down and paint benches, re-paint markings on the sidewalks and sand &amp; paint pillars all around the school.

This is only the beginning of a long partnership with The United Way for One Park Financial. Together they aim to build a #StrongerMiami and give back to the city.
